Equity

The foundation asset class — three modules covering ownership, market structure, and corporate actions.

₹60,000 Core Allocation Platform: Zerodha Kite 3 Modules

Equity is the first asset class in the curriculum, and the one your ₹60,000 core allocation sits in. This hub covers ownership mechanics, how the market is structured above the level of a single stock, and what happens to your holding when a company takes a corporate action. Read in order — Core Mechanics before the other two, since everything downstream assumes you know what a share and a settlement cycle actually are.
