₹20,000 Options Allocation Platform: Zerodha Kite Buy Only — No Naked Selling

Options are contracts whose value derives entirely from an underlying equity or index — everything here assumes you already know what a share is, how the index is built, and how settlement works. Four resources exist for this track, each covering different ground; read them in the order below rather than jumping around.

Prerequisite: Equity — Core Mechanics & Market Structure. Options pricing (Delta, the options chain, index moneyness) doesn't make sense without the underlying mechanics first.
