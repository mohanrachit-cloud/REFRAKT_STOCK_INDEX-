The Framework
How to Pick a Stock — The 4-Step Filter
Use This Order Every Time. No Exceptions.
STEP 1 · SCREEN
Quality Filter
ROE >15%, ROCE >15%, D/E <1. Kills 80% of bad stocks in 2 minutes on Screener.in.
STEP 2 · VALUE
Valuation Check
P/E vs sector median. P/B vs historical average. Cheap for a reason — or genuinely undervalued?
STEP 3 · VERIFY
Cash Flow Audit
OCF must track Net Profit. Growing profit + declining OCF = manipulation signal. Never skip this.
STEP 4 · TIME
TA Entry
Open TradingView. FA gave you the WHAT. TA gives you the WHEN. Great company, wrong entry = painful loss.
